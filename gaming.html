Slots & Electronic Gaming at Magic Palace – Over 400 Machines Ready to Play

Magic Palace brings you one of the largest slot machine selections in the Montréal area. With over 400 electronic machines on the floor, our Kahnawake gaming lounge is a go-to destination for players looking to enjoy everything from classic slot games to the latest multi-line video slots.

Located just minutes from downtown Montréal, Magic Palace offers an exciting, easy-access experience for anyone who enjoys slots and electronic gaming.

Play Your Favourite Slot Machines

Our slot floor is packed with variety — from timeless single-line machines to cutting-edge video slots with themed bonuses and free spin features.

Our slot machine selection includes:

  • Classic reel slots
  • Multi-line video slots
  • Branded slots with movie & music themes
  • Penny slots, quarter machines, and high-limit options
  • Machines with random and progressive jackpots

We rotate new titles in regularly to keep things fresh and exciting, and all machines are tested and maintained to high standards.

Loyalty Program for Slot Players

Frequent slot players can earn even more with our free Loyalty Program. Get access to:

  • Member-exclusive slot machine promotions
  • Bonus credits
  • Entry into monthly draws
  • Priority access to new machine launches

Sign up at the front desk and start earning from your first spin.

Jackpot Slot Machines & Progressives

Chase big wins with our progressive slot machines. Linked across multiple terminals, these games build up major jackpots that can be triggered at random or with the right combination.

Progressives are clearly marked on the floor, and staff are happy to help explain how they work.

Comfortable, Modern Slot Lounge

The layout at Magic Palace is designed for a smooth and enjoyable experience. Machines are spaced out to give you room to play comfortably, with easy access to drinks, washrooms, and dining nearby.

Our facility is fully air-conditioned, cleaned continuously, and includes a bar and restaurant just steps away.

Responsible Slot Play

Magic Palace supports responsible gaming. If you’d like information on safe slot machine play or wish to take a break, just speak to a staff member at any time.
