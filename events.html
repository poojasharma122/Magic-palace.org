Events at Magic Palace – Something Happening Every Week

Looking for more than just gameplay? Magic Palace hosts a full calendar of events designed to bring players together for fun, rewards, and live entertainment.

From themed casino nights to slot tournaments and prize draws, our events add something extra to your visit.

Upcoming Events

🎰 Monthly Slot Tournaments

Test your luck and timing in our monthly slot tournaments. With cash prizes, leaderboard standings, and bonus credit for top players, it’s a crowd favourite. Registration is free for Loyalty members.

🎟 Spin & Win Weekends

Every weekend, random machines across the floor are picked for surprise credit drops. Keep an eye on the displays and signage — your next spin might trigger a bonus.

🍻 Live Music Fridays

Join us every Friday night for live music in the lounge. Grab a drink, unwind, and enjoy the atmosphere while staying close to the gaming floor.

🎂 Birthday Month Bonuses

Celebrate with us! If it’s your birthday month, you’ll receive free play credit and entry into our birthday draw. Just present ID at the Loyalty desk.

🎄 Seasonal Specials

EHoliday weekends and seasonal events come with their own bonus promotions, food specials, and themed games. Don’t miss out — they change fast.

Private Events & Group Bookings

Hosting a celebration, group night, or corporate event? Magic Palace offers packages for groups of all sizes with access to gaming, dining, and drinks under one roof.

Get in touch with us to plan:

  • Private tournament nights
  • Dining + gaming packages
  • Team building events
  • Birthday celebrations

Custom menus and reserved areas are available by request.
