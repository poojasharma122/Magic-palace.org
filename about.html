About Magic Palace – Kahnawake’s Go-To Gaming Spot Since 2002

Magic Palace is an independent, locally operated gaming destination based in Kahnawake, just minutes from Montréal’s South Shore. Since opening in 2002, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted venues for electronic slot gaming, casual dining, and player-focused promotions.

What We Offer

Our venue features:

  • Over 400 electronic slot machines
  • Regular jackpot opportunities
  • A relaxed on-site restaurant and bar
  • Daily promotions and player rewards
  • Free on-site parking and easy highway access

We keep things simple: good games, fair play, and a reliable experience every day of the week.

Why Players Choose Us

Magic Palace is built around comfort, ease, and real value for players. From the layout of our machines to the late-night dining menu, every detail is designed to make your visit smooth and enjoyable.

Whether you’re stopping by for a short session or making it a full night out, you’ll find everything you need under one roof.

Our Location

Located in the heart of Kahnawake, Magic Palace is just a short drive from downtown Montréal, Longueuil, and surrounding South Shore communities. We’re easily accessible by car, with plenty of free parking available on site.

A Local, Independent Operation

We’re not part of a major chain. Magic Palace is fully locally owned and operated, with a team that knows the community and takes pride in offering a consistent, no-pressure experience.

Our staff are approachable, trained, and here to help — no matter how experienced (or new) you are to the floor.

Commitment to Responsible Play

We support healthy, informed gaming. Responsible Play resources, information pamphlets, and assistance are available on site at all times. Our goal is to provide a safe, respectful space for all visitors.
