Privacy Policy

Magic Palace (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you access magic-palace.org (“Site”) or engage with our services. Please read this document carefully to understand our practices and your rights

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Sign up for newsletters or marketing communications
  • Submit inquiries via contact forms
  • Participate in promotions or events
  • Create or manage accounts (where applicable)

Typical personal data includes your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details.

Automatically Collected Data:

When you visit our Site, certain technical data is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and duration of visit
  • Referral sources

This data helps us understand user behavior and improve Site functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Site and services
  • To communicate with you, including responding to inquiries and sending newsletters, promotions, and updates (only if you opt-in)
  • To personalise content and tailor your experience
  • To administer contests, promotions, or events
  • To monitor and analyse Site usage for performance and security
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Magic Palace uses cookies and tracking tools to enhance user experience and Site performance. Cookies may be session-based or persistent and assist in remembering user preferences, tracking Site usage, and enabling certain Site functions.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may impact Site functionality.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with:

  • Trusted service providers who perform functions on our behalf (e.g., email delivery, analytics)
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Third parties in connection with a business transfer, merger, or acquisition, subject to confidentiality agreements

All third parties with access to your information are obligated to maintain its confidentiality and security.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or outdated data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Request data portability, where applicable
  • File a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Site and services are intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us to request removal.

8. International Users

If you access the Site from outside Canada, please be aware that your information may be processed in Canada and other jurisdictions where Magic Palace or its service providers operate. Data protection laws may vary by location.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out to:
