Personal Data Policy

Magic Palace (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Personal Data Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the personal data you provide when using our services, including through magic-palace.org (“Site”).

1. What Is Personal Data?

Personal data refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. This includes details such as your name, contact information, email address, IP address, and any other data that can identify you directly or indirectly.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect personal data when you:

  • Register for newsletters, promotions, or loyalty programs
  • Submit inquiries or feedback
  • Participate in events or contests
  • Use our website and services

Data may be collected directly from you or through automated means such as cookies and tracking technologies.

3. Purpose of Processing Personal Data

Your personal data is processed to:

  • Provide, personalise, and improve our services
  • Communicate important information including newsletters, promotions, and updates (only if you consent)
  • Manage and administer contests, loyalty programs, and events
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Your consent when you opt-in for communications or services
  • Performance of contracts related to services you request
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Our legitimate interests, such as improving services and security, balanced with your rights and freedoms

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or to comply with legal requirements. When data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell personal data to third parties. Personal data may be shared with:

  • Trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Third parties involved in business transactions, with safeguards in place

Transfers of data outside Canada are conducted only with appropriate protections to ensure data security.

7. Your Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Accessing your data
  • Requesting correction or deletion
  • Withdrawing consent to marketing communications
  • Objecting to or restricting certain processing
  • Requesting data portability
  • Filing complaints with supervisory authorities

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@magic-palace.org.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our Site with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

For questions or requests about this policy or your personal data, please contact:

Email: info@magic-palace.org

Address: Magic Palace, Route 132, Kahnawake, QC
